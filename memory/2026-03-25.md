### 4. Uncertainty Costs More Than Delay — Chemical Logistics Digital Corridor Coordination
- Chemical logistics costs = 15–25% of product value. Uncertainty is more damaging than predictable delay: unpredictable arrivals force excess inventory, reserved "just-in-case" capacity, and backup premiums — all pure waste.
- Digital corridor coordination (shared visibility across actors without exposing competitive data) is the structural fix. Low-carbon fuel complexity intensifies coordination requirements.
- **Quantix action:** Terminal-to-truck handoff is exactly this problem. Even a 2-hour truck delay causes loading resequencing cascade if terminal doesn't know. TMS/portal approach for shared visibility across Quantix's 50+ terminals is the implementation path.
- Source: Maritime Executive / Lind, Lehmacher, Bentham et al., February 20, 2026
