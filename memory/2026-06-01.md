### Insight 1: Agentic AI Splitting SC Software Into Two Distinct Layers (Kognitos, May 26, 2026)
**Core finding:** The most practically significant mid-2026 SC AI insight is architectural. SC AI is actually two separate technology problems:
- **Layer 1 — Planning & Execution:** Blue Yonder, o9, Kinaxis, SAP IBP, Oracle SCM — mature platforms; AI adds optimization on top of existing engines. Not a buying confusion problem.
- **Layer 2 — Operational Workflow Automation:** BOLs, freight invoice audit, customs docs, PO ack, vendor exceptions, statement reconciliation. Historically underserved by RPA. Agentic AI (Kognitos, IBM Sterling + watsonx, Microsoft D365 Copilot agents, MarkIt) is the 2026 disruptor here.
- Key finding: **Layer 2 has the highest short-term ROI** — benchmark: Century Supply Chain processes 50,000+ BOLs/month on agentic AI automation.
- Regulatory compliance (hazmat chain of custody) = deterministic AI (not generative) is the right architecture for BOL/SDS workflows.
**Quantix relevance:** High — Quantix generates high-volume transactional documents. Layer 2 workflow automation is a scoping-ready near-term target.
**Promotion decision:** PROMOTED to MEMORY.md — new architectural framing (Layer 1 vs. Layer 2) not previously captured; actionable near-term direction for Dale's AI-in-the-loop build.

### Insight 2: Predictive Maintenance Now Table-Stakes for Fleet Ops (FleetRabbit, May 30, 2026)
**Core finding:**
- AI predictive maintenance embedded in Samsara, Geotab, Motive — no data science team required.
- Reported outcomes: 35–40% fewer unplanned breakdowns; 50–60% maintenance cost reduction; uptime 87% (2021 avg) → 94–96% (2026).
- Unit economics: Single prevented Class 8 breakdown = 8–15 hours downtime + $2K–$4K emergency repair costs avoided. 48–72 hour advance notice enables planned maintenance windows.
- Satellite connectivity (Starlink/OneWeb): Real-time connectivity eliminates 2–8 hour data delays in cellular dead zones. Edge computing on-vehicle for critical events.
- Compliance automation (eDVIR, HOS, digital audit records) now legal baseline, not differentiator.
- Emissions as contract requirement: Major shippers (Amazon, Walmart, DHL, UPS) making decarbonization a contract term.
**Quantix relevance:** At 4,500+ trucks, 1% uptime improvement = ~45 additional truck-days/month capacity. Satellite connectivity relevant for rural bulk chemical routes in TX/LA corridors.
**Promotion decision:** NOT promoted — incremental confirmation of existing OTA/telematics/fleet management entries. Unit economics benchmarks documented here for reference.
